How to put YouTube videos on a page

Find a video on YouTube that you want to add to your page and click the share button underneath the video.

If you wish, you can check the box that says start, this box will allow you to choose a starting point when users click play.

When you are ready simply, copy the code at the top and paste it where you want it to appear on your page.

Take note:

YouTube offers different options when embedding a video.

When embedding a video you have the option to show controls.

You can choose to show suggested videos when the video finishes.

You can also choose to omit player controls. Clicking on the video player will pause or play the video.

You can also choose to enable a privacy enhanced-mode. YouTube wont store information about visitors on your website unless they play the video.
